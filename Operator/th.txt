There are three types of datatypes .
1. unary 
    - it will required only one operand
    ex : a++ ++a 
2. binary 
    - it will required two operand

3. ternary
    - it will required three operands


Binary operator :
1. Airthmatic operator : + , - , * , / , % , ** 
2. Assignment operator : = , += , -= , *= , %= 
3. relation operator / comparision operator : == , < , > , <= , >= , != , === , !==
4. logical operator : && , || , !
5. bitwise operator : & , |
